About Us

_______________________________________________________


The Township Blue Sox is a community-focused baseball organization dedicated to developing youth talent and promoting teamwork and sportsmanship. Founded with the mission of providing a competitive and inclusive environment for young athletes, the Blue Sox offers a range of programs tailored for players of all ages and skill levels, from beginners to advanced competitors.


With a strong emphasis on sportsmanship and personal growth, the organization encourages players to not only enhance their baseball skills but also develop important life lessons in leadership, resilience, and cooperation. Regular practices, games, and tournaments are integral to the Blue Sox experience, allowing players to showcase their talents and build lasting friendships.


Community support and volunteer involvement are vital to the Blue Sox's success, as local families and businesses come together to create a vibrant sports culture. The organization also prioritizes creating a positive and safe environment for all participants, ensuring that every player feels valued and inspired to reach their full potential. As a cherished part of the local sports landscape, the Township Blue Sox continues to leave a lasting impact on its players and the broader community.

TOWNSHIP BLUE SOX COACHES



The Township Blue Sox are looking for coaches to join us for our upcoming 2024-2025 season. The season start with our fall ball program in Sept. & Oct. We then move into our off season winter training that runs for 6-7 weeks in Nov. & Dec. Tryouts will start early in the new year with our teams being chosen in early Feb.


We are offering teams in the following divisions for 2025:

    13U AAA

    15U AA

    15U AAA

    18U AAA


Up to this point stability at the township level has been a challenge. We strive to change that going forward. We are looking to name our head coaches for our 2025 teams in Aug. so that they can begin the development process in the fall and work with players through the off season. When we pick our teams in February the coaches will have already worked with their players for months building clear expectations and a development path.


If you are interested in coaching with our Township Blue Sox teams please go to the contact section. Please have your application in by July 25th to be considered. Coach selections will be announced on Aug. 2nd.
